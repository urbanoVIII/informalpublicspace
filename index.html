This website is the result of an ongoing research about Public Space in Informal Settlements that the Interamerican Development Bank and Ecosistema Urbano began in 2019.


The research project is articulated into 4 main branches:

1

An introduction to the research topic

2

A repository of georeferenced case studies related to the topic.

3

An interpretation of the case studies research results.

4

A closer scale view of a sample of existing informal settlements in Argentina.
